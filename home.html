Our Speciality

Our Apple Pancake is a single large pancake smothered with sautéed apples and cinnamon sugar baked to perfection, to create a deliciously rich cinnamon sugar glaze.

The Dutch Baby is an air filled delight oven baked to golden perfection and served with lemon, whipped butter and powdered sugar to create a mouthwatering blend.

Our unique omelets are rolled in a skillet and then oven baked to give you a splendidly light, moist delicacy.

Our gourmet crepes include the cherry kijafa crepe, a blend of tart cherries and sweet Danish cherry wine.

What makes our pancakes special?

Our recipes demand only the very finest of ingredients, such as 93 score butter, pure 36% whipping cream, fresh grade AA eggs, hard wheat unbleached flour, and our own recipe sourdough starter. Our batters and sauces are made fresh in each restaurant’s kitchen.

Old-fashioned sourdough yeast, just like your great grandmother used to use, is used in all our pancake batters. It is grown in our own kitchen from a culture of potatoes, flour and sugar, activated by a "start of yeast". The sourdough yeast gives light, fine-textured pancakes.

We've been serving you since 1953!

The Original Pancake House was founded in Portland, Oregon in 1953 by Les Highet and Erma Hueneke. Drawing upon their many years of experience in the culinary field, and their extensive working knowledge of authentic national and ethnic pancake recipes they were able to offer without compromise this unique and original menu which has gained national acclaim.

We now have over one hundred franchises from coast to coast, which have won local and national acclaim.
